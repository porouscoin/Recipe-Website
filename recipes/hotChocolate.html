Ingredients
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
Steps
  1. Heat 1 cup of milk in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Mix in cocoa powder, sugar, and salt.
  3. Stir until completely dissolved.
  4. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.
  6. Pour into a cup and enjoy!
